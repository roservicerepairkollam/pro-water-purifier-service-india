<h2>Why Does Your RO Make Noise? Common Causes</h2>

<p>A well-maintained RO system operates quietly. Here are the common reasons your <strong>RO making noise</strong>:</p>

<p><strong>Humming or whining:</strong> Often caused by air trapped in the check valve, especially after installation. Try flushing the system (closing the tank valve and letting water run through the spigot for a minute) to release the air.</p>

<p><strong>Gurgling or dripping:</strong> This is normal reject water flowing to the drain. If it's too loud, check the drain line adapter or install a backflow preventer.</p>

<p><strong>High-pitched whine:</strong> This could be membrane failure, especially if the pre-filters haven't been changed regularly (every 6 months).</p>

<p><strong>Severe vibration:</strong> Usually caused by incorrect line installation.</p>

<p>If the noise persists after basic troubleshooting, call a professional to check the membrane or check valve.</p>

<h2>RO Membrane Lifespan & When to Replace</h2>

<p>Understanding <a href="https://www.rocareindia.com/ro-water-purifier-service-east-godawari">ro membrane lifespan</a> is crucial for maintaining water quality. A membrane typically lasts 2-5 years in a home system, but can be shorter in hard water areas like East Godavari.</p>

<h3>Signs Your Membrane Is Failing</h3>

<p><strong>Rising TDS in purified water:</strong> Check with a TDS meter.</p>

<p><strong>Reduced water flow:</strong> From the faucet.</p>

<p><strong>Water tastes off:</strong> Or leaves scale spots.</p>

<p><strong>System hums loudly:</strong> Indicating the pump is overworking due to a clogged membrane.</p>

<h3>Factors That Shorten Lifespan</h3>

<p><strong>High TDS or hard water:</strong> Common in East Godavari.</p>

<p><strong>Poor filter maintenance:</strong> Pre-filters must be changed every 6-12 months.</p>

<p><strong>Chlorine exposure:</strong> Damages the membrane quickly.</p>

<p>In industrial systems, membranes can last 3-7 years with proper pretreatment. With the right care, you can maximize yours.</p>
