<p><strong>High TDS output:</strong> Indicates the membrane is degrading.</p>
<p><strong>Noisy operation:</strong> Often means pump issues or air in the system.</p>
<p><strong>UV lamp failure:</strong> Means bacteria may not be killed.</p>
